Ingredients
-3/4 cup butter, melted
- 1 1/2 cups dark brown sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 1/4 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 2 teaspoons cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 2 cups instant oats
- 1/2 cup craisins
- 1/2 cup pecans

Preheat oven to 375 degrees F

Mix butter, sugar, eggs and vanilla. 

Add flour, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon and mix until smooth.

slowly add oats, craisins and pecans. 

drop dough on a pan by tablespoon separating a little more than an inch.

Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges look crisp and the very middle still looks soft and slightly doughy. 

Let cool for a few minutes minutes before removing from the pan.
